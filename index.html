Welcome!👋

The purpose of this tool is to help you navigate the wide-ranging and fast-changing COVID-19 restrictions around the world. Explore countries on the map or search for a specific country below!

⚠ The CDC and WHO recommend avoiding non-essential travel because of COVID-19. Please remember to wear a mask, wash your hands, 6 feet apart, and avoid crowds.⚠

Disclaimer: This tool does not intend to encourage people to travel, but rather help them with their travel arrangements. Travel restrictions are essential in reducing the spread of COVID-19. Please limit your travels as necessary for your and the public's safety.

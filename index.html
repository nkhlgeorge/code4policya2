COVID-19 Restrictions by Country

Welcome!👋

The purpose of this tool is to help you navigate the wide-ranging and fast-changing COVID-19 restrictions around the world. Explore countries on the map or search for a specific country below!

⚠ COVID-19 cases are rising world over. The CDC recommends avoiding non-essential travel. Please remember to wear a mask, wash your hands, stay 6 feet apart, and avoid crowds.⚠

Disclaimer: This tool does not intend to, by any means, encourage people to travel, but rather help them with their travel arrangements. Travel restrictions, and people’s adherence to essential trips are key elements in reducing the geographic spread of Covid. Limit your travels to the necessary ones only. When you have to, follow the protocol on hygiene, face covering, social distancing and quarantine, for the public’s and your own safety.
